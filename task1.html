Introduction

Nature refers to the physical world and everything that is not made by humans. It encompasses a wide range of living and non-living entities.

Biodiversity

Biodiversity is the variety of life forms on Earth, including plants, animals, fungi, and microorganisms. It plays a crucial role in maintaining ecological balance.

Ecosystems

An ecosystem is a community of living organisms in conjunction with non-living components of their environment. Examples include forests, oceans, and deserts.
